Our Trainings

We aim to organize different kinds of weekly trainings which are suitable for every level. It is all for free thanks to our wonderful volunteers!

Speed Session

Tuesday

Track sessions for improving pace and techique. Includes warm-up and stretching in the end.

Park Run

Saturday

Social 2.5km/5km run in Baitang Park with coffee and chat in Starbucks after the run.

Long Run

Sunday

Average 12-15km weekly run to improve aerobic endurance. Routes are changing weekly.

Trail Run

Thursday

Running on hills and exploring new places in Suzhou and around it. Great way to keep fir, socialize and enjoy nature.

About us

First of all, we are community and we prioritize friendship and support over competition. We enjoy training and taking part in running races as well as organzing events and celebrations to strengthen bonds between all striders.

The community was founded by two passionate runners - Michael Gates and Booker Liu in 2018. They had an idea to connect chinese and foreigners through running. From the beginning all the events and trainings we hold are bilingual. We are international running community, and our goal to keep fit as well as make friend from around the world, improve language skills and have fun.
